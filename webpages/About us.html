Mug and Mingle is a local specialty coffee shop, which specalises in a unique, high-quality coffee.This coffee shop uses high-end products to cater to all our enthusiasts who love coffee! Mug and Mingle was founded in 2005, with the aim to make a coffee shop with unique coffee flavours. we allow our customers to create their own coffee. we are the first coffee shop to let the customers create their own coffee which has allowed us to create werid and wonderful drinks for our enthusiasts. we also sell cakes that are made every single day, we always ask our customers what type of cake they would like to try next!
